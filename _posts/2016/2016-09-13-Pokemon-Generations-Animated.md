The Pokémon Company International has announced an animated Pokémon Generations series that start on September 18th at 9am PST on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/user/pokemon). There will be a total of 18 episodes each running between 3 and 5 minutes long, with the first two episodes airing on the 16th.

The Generations series takes a look back at each generation of the Pokémon video games and tell stories from each region from Kanto to Kalos. There's plenty of new faces among familiar ones, and the stories told will be deeper than ones told before, really giving you a look into the life and world of Pokémon.
