<span class="large-cap">A</span>t Pavilion, I'm primarily working on a complete site <em>redesign</em>. Here you'll find a few images of what is currently (9/26/2016) a work in progress. The screenshots below are all taken in-browser (which is why there are some spacing/content glitches).
